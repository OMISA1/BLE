OMISA

Open Modular Interchangeable Sensor Architecture — Bluetooth interface
Bluetooth connection
Your phone keeps its normal WiFi and internet — OMISA connects over Bluetooth only. Nothing is shared with OMISA and no network details are needed.
Not connected.
This browser cannot use Bluetooth.
Web Bluetooth works in Chrome on Android and desktop Chrome. It is not available on iPhone or iPad, or in Safari and Firefox.
Every device can use the WiFi interface instead
  1. Open your WiFi settings and join the network OMISA (password omisa1234).
  2. Open a browser and go to http://omisa.local — or http://192.168.4.1 if that does not load.
  3. If your phone warns that the network has no internet, choose to stay connected.
While connected this way your phone uses OMISA's own network, so it will not have internet until you switch back. Both interfaces show the same live data.
